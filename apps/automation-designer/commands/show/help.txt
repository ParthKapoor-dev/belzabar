Usage: belz show-method <uuid> [flags]

Displays details of an Automation Designer method.
It will automatically fetch and cache the method if not present locally or if the cache is stale (5 mins).

Arguments:
  <uuid>                The UUID of the method (chain) to display.

Flags:
  --inputs              Show a table of input arguments.
  --services            Show a list of service steps.
  --service-detail <n>  Show detailed info for a specific service step (0-indexed).
  --full                Include expanded per-service logic details.
  --raw                 Include raw payloads in output data.
  --force               Ignore cache and force a fresh fetch from the API.
  --help, -h            Show this help message.

Examples:
  belz show-method 1234-5678 --inputs
  belz show-method 1234-5678 --services
  belz show-method 1234-5678 --service-detail 2 --llm
  belz show-method 1234-5678 --service-detail 2 --raw --llm
  belz show-method 1234-5678 --force
