Usage: belz fetch-method <UUID> [flags]

Fetches details for a specific Automation Method by its UUID.
Automatically resolves Draft vs Published IDs.

Arguments:
  <UUID>    The unique identifier for the method (Draft or Published).

Flags:
  --raw     Include raw method payload in output.
  --help, -h    Show this help message.

Example:
  belz fetch-method 123e4567-e89b-12d3-a456-426614174000
  belz fetch-method 123e4567-e89b-12d3-a456-426614174000 --raw --llm
