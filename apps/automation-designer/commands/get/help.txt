Usage: belz ad get <UUID> [flags]

Fetch an Automation Designer method by its UUID (draft or published).

Arguments:
  <UUID>              The unique identifier of the method to fetch.

Flags:
  --raw               Include raw method payload in output.
  --help, -h          Show this help message.

Global Flags:
  --env <name>        Set active environment. (default: nsm-dev)
                      Available: nsm-dev | nsm-qa | nsm-uat
  --llm               Output structured JSON envelope for scripting/LLM use.

Examples:
  belz ad get 123e4567-e89b-12d3-a456-426614174000
  belz ad get 123e4567-e89b-12d3-a456-426614174000 --raw
  belz ad get 123e4567-e89b-12d3-a456-426614174000 --env nsm-qa --llm
