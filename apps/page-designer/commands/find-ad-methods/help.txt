Usage: pd find-ad-methods <ID> [flags]

Lists all Automation Designer (AD) methods referenced in a page or component.

Arguments:
  <ID>          The UUID of the page, or Name of the component.

Flags:
  --recursive, -r   Deep scan through all child components.
  --component       Treat the <ID> as a Component Name instead of a Page ID.
  --help, -h        Show this help message.

Example:
  pd find-ad-methods 49fa2576...
  pd find-ad-methods n_s_staff_email_correspondence --component --recursive
