belz ad categories [flags]

List all AD categories (services) — both user-generated and system-generated.

Flags:
  --user     Only show USER_GENERATED categories
  --system   Only show SYSTEM_GENERATED categories
  --llm      Emit a JSON envelope with {total, filter, categories[]}

Example:
  belz ad categories --user
  belz ad categories --llm | jq '.data.categories[] | select(.type == "SYSTEM_GENERATED")'
