belz ad child-info <category> <method-name> [flags]

Fetch metadata for a method referenced by name from another method's step
(e.g. when a step calls "Helpers.Legacy.echo" you can look up the child's
inputs/outputs without already knowing its UUID).

Backs GET /rest/api/automation-systems/{category}/automation-apis/{method-name}.

Flags:
  --llm    Emit a JSON envelope

Example:
  belz ad child-info Helpers.Legacy echo
